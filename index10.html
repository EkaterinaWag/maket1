Welcome to Monumental

April,25 2013Posted by Someone
Welcome to our Residential. The Monumental Residential is located at Estrada Monumental, in the heart of the main tourist area of Funchal.It has 24 rooms, all with private bathroom, telephone, free wireless internet, safe, cable TV, laundry service, 24-hour reception service and balconies with panoramic sea views. It has the support of the Chanceller restaurant and the Granny's House restaurant, where you can have breakfast and enjoy a wide variety of regional and international food.
